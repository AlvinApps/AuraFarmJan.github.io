1. Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require you to create accounts or provide personal information
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other contact information
  • We do not collect precise location data (GPS coordinates)
  • We do not access your photos, contacts, camera, or other personal files
  • We do not record or store any audio or music from your device

Information Automatically Collected

When you use our App, the following information may be automatically collected through our advertising partner (Google AdMob) and app analytics:

Device Information:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Screen resolution and device settings
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA/AAID)
  • IP address (used only to determine general geographic region)
  • Device performance metrics (for game optimization)

Game Usage Information:

  • Game sessions and play duration
  • Coins collected and scores achieved
  • Game modes played (race mode, free driving mode)
  • Boat navigation patterns and gameplay statistics
  • App screens visited and session duration
  • Date and time of app usage
  • App crashes and performance data
  • Game progression and achievements

Advertising Data:

  • Ad interactions and preferences
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies used by ad networks

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: To provide and maintain the boat dancing adventure gaming experience
  • Game Improvement: To optimize boat controls, coin collection mechanics, and game modes
  • Performance Optimization: To ensure smooth sailing and dancing animations
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with different game modes and improve the App
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do not use your information for marketing communications since we do not collect contact information.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Google AdMob and other trusted service providers who help operate our App
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

  • Google Play Services: Google Play Services Privacy Policy

These services may collect additional information as described in their respective privacy policies.

5. Your Privacy Choices

Advertising Controls

  • Reset Advertising ID: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID
  • Opt out of Personalized Ads: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS Users: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads (toggle off)

App Controls

  • Uninstall: Remove the App to stop all data collection
  • Device Permissions: Review and modify any permissions granted to the App in your device settings
  • Game Data Reset: Uninstalling and reinstalling the app will reset all locally stored game progress

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Game Data: Our App stores minimal game data (coins, scores, progress) locally on your device, which is deleted when you uninstall the App
  • Third-Party Data: Data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's data retention policies
  • Deletion Requests: To request deletion of data associated with your advertising ID, follow Google's instructions or contact us at the email below
  • Local Storage: All boat customizations, coin collections, and game achievements are stored locally and removed upon app uninstallation

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our App is suitable for all ages, including children. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Advertisements shown to users are family-friendly and appropriate for all ages
  • The dancing boat theme is designed to be fun and appropriate for children
  • Parents can contact us with questions about their child's use of the App
  • If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly
